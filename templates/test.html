Mission Statement

As a lifelong area resident, Graduate of SUNY at Binghamton and BBC, including 25 years of public service and a NYS Licensed Driving Instructor, our main goal at Vic's Pro-Driving Shool is

To instruct our students to know more than what is needed to pass a road test, and most importantly, to become a very safe, skilled and attentive driver in all situations.

Vic's Pro-Driving School serves the Greater Binghamton Area as well as Broome, Tioga and Chenango Counties.

Our Driver training vehicles are late model Honda Accords, dual control equipped and very well maintained for your pleasure and safety.

Learning to drive with Vic's Pro-Driving School will give you the freedom you need to succeed.

Victor A. Prokop, Owner
